~Don't overthink it, just lift it~

Bench

  • Get a proper arch
  • Tuck your elbows
  • Bend the bar on the way down, pull it apart on the way up
  • At the end of the setup, move you butt a bit back while setting up your feet

Squat

  • Knees out!
  • Stay upright, proud chest
  • Proper bar position on your back
  • Bend the bar
  • Breathe in belly before unracking, dont exhale before flexing glutes after walkout
  • Break from hips
  • Squeeze glutes, hips forwards, before starting descent

Deadlift

  • Initialise with legs
  • Sit back more
  • Push the ground away instead of pulling the bar up
